About

The conference is a single day event organised by both students and feminists living in the town. The events will range from gender and class, women and mental health, gendered homelessness, ecofeminism, feminist history, female refugees/ asylum seekers and more! We hope to provide platforms especially to those who emphasise the interrelation of oppressions and call for united action.

In a time when feminism is becoming increasingly high profile, and in some spaces, increasingly liberal in its demands, we want to stage a day of discussions and workshops that go beyond identity politics and assimilation. Feminism in Theory and Action will focus strongly on relocating feminism within everyday political struggles.
